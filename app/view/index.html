Kombucha Mold Detector

Upload a picture of your Kombucha brew to determine if your SCOBY is healthy or moldy

Methodology


This website was created for my Capstone in the Artificial Intelligence Guild Apprentice Program.

The Kombucha Mold Detector is a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) Classifier built via ResNet18 with transfer learning through the fast.ai library. Using the brightness and color of individual pixels as input features, CNN classifiers ("Computer Vision models") utilize the brains of a neural network to identify granular shapes and textures within images and ultimately understand their relationship to the target variable (in this case, the presense of Mold). The model was trained on images scraped from various Kombucha blogs and other photos labeled from credible sources.

Prediction Accuracy rate: 88.9%.

Prediction Recall rate: 87.7%.
